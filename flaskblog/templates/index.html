Who we are?

Lamp of Cheer is a voluntary and accountable organisation which has been created for students' mental health. Now-a-days mental illness is a big issue all over the world. Most of the people are unconscious about mental health. In our country a lot of people are suffering from mental problems such as depression, frustration,anxiety and so on.All most 8000,000 people die due to suicide every year all over the world. Every 40 seconds,one person commits suicide.The number of committing suicide from 10 years old to 29 years old is 22,093 in 2016. From this 52,750 people are from 15 to 19 years old .79% suicide cases occurred in low and middle income countries in 2016. In 2016 suicide was the 18th leading cause of death.
Asia accounts for 60% of the world suicide. Close to 60 million people are affected by suicide or attempt suicide in Asia each year.(Beautrais 2006)
In Bangladesh, from September, 2019 to January 2020 , all most 11 thousand people committed suicide.(Source: Bangladesh Bureau of Statistics)

Do students need to pay to receive services?

In case of necessarily working with experts, students will need to pay a fair amount. In other cases, they will just need to participate in our ECA related activities instead.

  • Will the information of students be kept private?

    The identity & information of students won't be disclosed to anyone. It's our determination to help with the development of mental health's condition while ensuring your privacy fully.

  • Will there be any experts to deal with student's problems?

    Yes, there will be.

  • Will the activities be online or offline?

    There will be both online & offline activities. We'll be arranging offline workshop sessions & online activities. Most of the activities will be arranged both online & offline.
